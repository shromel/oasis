# Full Body Workout — 4x/Week
**Baseline:** 3 pullups / 4 dips / 25 pushups / 60s plank / 10 knee raises
**Split:** 🏖️ Outdoor (bar) + 🏠 Home (floor) — same day, any gap between is fine

---

## Every Session — Warm-Up

**🏖️ Before outdoor:**
- [ ] Scapular pulls on bar — 2x8 (hang, shrug shoulders DOWN, no arm bend)
- [ ] Arm + wrist circles — 30 sec each
- [ ] Pike pushup hold at bottom — 20 sec

**🏠 Before home:**
- [ ] 10 slow deep squats
- [ ] 10 slow pushups

---

## ━━━ LEVEL 1 — Foundation ━━━
**Exit when:** 8 pullups / 10 dips / 35 pushups / 90s plank

### 🏖️ Outdoor
| # | Exercise | Sets x Reps | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Pullups | 5x2 | Alternate: chin-ups (underhand) every other session |
| 2 | Negatives | 2x1 | After pullups — jump to top, 5 sec down |
| 3 | Inverted rows | 3x8 | Body 45°, squeeze shoulder blades at top |
| 4 | Dips | 3x3 | Elbows BACK, slight forward lean |
| 5 | Pike pushups | 3x8 | Hips high, lower head toward floor |
| 6 | Twisting knee raises | 3x8 per side | Hang, knees up, rotate to each side controlled |
| 7 | Leg raises | max after each set of #6 | Straight legs, whatever you can manage |

### 🏠 Home
| # | Exercise | Sets x Reps | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Pushups | 4x15 | Chest to floor, elbows 45°, squeeze chest at top |
| 2 | Squats deep | 3x20 | Slow and intentional |
| 3 | Side plank | 3x30 sec per side | Obliques |
| 4 | Plank elbows | 3x45 sec | |
| 5 | Single-leg calf raises | 3x12 per leg | Off a step, full range |

**Progression rule:** hit top of rep range 2 sessions in a row → add 1 rep next session.

## Muscle → Best Exercise Map

| Muscle | Exercise | Introduced |
|---|---|---|
| Lats | Pullups (wide grip) | Level 1 |
| Biceps | Chin-ups (underhand) | Level 1, alternate sessions |
| Triceps | Dips (upright torso) | Level 1 |
| Chest | Pushups → decline → archer → pseudo planche | Level 1→4 |
| Front delts | Pike pushups → wall HSPU → freestanding HSPU | Level 1→4 |
| Side delts | Wall HSPU wide hands | Level 2→4 |
| Rear delts + rhomboids | Inverted rows (horizontal) | Level 1→4 |
| Abs | Leg raises → toes-to-bar | Level 1→4 |
| Obliques | Twisting knee raises → windshield wipers | Level 1→4 |
| Quads | Squats → Bulgarian → pistol → weighted | Level 1→4 |
| Glutes | Bulgarian split squats + squats deep | Level 1→4 |
| Calves | Single-leg calf raises off step | Level 1→4 |
