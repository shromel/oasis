**IconTile** — rounded medallion that frames an icon. Used in the nav active state, list-row leading icons and empty-state badges.

```jsx
<IconTile tone="gold" glow><i data-lucide="scan-barcode" /></IconTile>
<IconTile size={44}><i data-lucide="dumbbell" /></IconTile>
```

- `tone`: `sunken` (default) · `gold` · `success`
- `size`: px (default 44) · `glow`: warm gold halo.
